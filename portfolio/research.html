Research

From the summer of 2018 to the spring of 2019, I worked on a research project in Dr. Stuart Smith's lab in the UNC Charlotte's Center for Precision Metrology with Kumar Aramugan to decrease the expense and size of interfermoters. Our goal is to piezo electric plates to compress and expand a crystal, bending the beam of a laser interfermoter to achieve the modulation necessary for precision interferometry.
